Ferrari

Ferrari is a super sports car manufacturer based in Maranello, province of Modena, in the Emilia-Romagna region of Italy. This company is the result of the idea of ​​its founder Enzo Ferrari to create a brand of passenger cars with luxurious and sporty features, based on the successes obtained from the creation of his motorsport Scuderia Ferrari in 1929.


Some of their best cars are:


Ferari 166m

Not everyone knows the Ferrari 166 MM. It is one of their best classics, a model that was based on the Ferrari 125 S. Manufactured in 1948, it pays tribute to the Mille Miglia, hence the two M's at the end of the name.


Thanks to a powerful V12 engine, it was capable of developing 140 horsepower, which allowed the car to declare a top speed of 200 kilometers per hour.
